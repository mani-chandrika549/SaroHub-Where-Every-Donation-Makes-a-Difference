Daily Challenges

30-Day Donation Challenge

Motto: “Small coins, big change.”

Donate ₹1 per day for 30 days. Build a consistent giving habit. Participants who complete all 30 days will be acknowledged for their dedication!

365-Day Kindness Challenge

Motto: “A rupee a day keeps indifference away.”

Commit to donating ₹1 daily for a full year. Your consistency supports countless causes. Finishers will receive a certificate of recognition from SARO.

90-Day Consistency Challenge

Motto: “Drop by drop, the bucket fills.”

Take the pledge to donate ₹2 daily for 90 days. It's not about the amount—it's about consistency and intention. Top consistent givers will be featured!
