About Saro Connect

Who We Are: Saro Connect is a community-driven initiative by SRIT students under the Save A Rupee Organization. We aim to empower individuals to bring positive change to society through innovative solutions and impactful actions.

Our Mission: To inspire and mobilize people to contribute towards societal betterment, fostering collaboration and service-oriented projects.

Our Vision: To create a sustainable and inclusive society where every effort counts, and every individual feels empowered to make a difference.
